Renew your passport online via the website
Beneficiary category
  • Jordanian citizens
Service places
  • Via the website
  • or visit the Civil Status and Passports Department
Conditions for obtaining the service
the required documents, and instructions
  • You must be registered on the website.
  • A copy of the old passport, civil ID and a personal photo must be attached.
  • You must make sure that the information is correct when filling out the application,
    otherwise you will face problems in the renewal process or it may be rejected
Documents required at the Civil Status Department office
to receive the new passport after obtaining approval
  • The old passport.
  • Proof of identity.
  • 50 JOD renewal fee.
